Overview

Copper Spoon Kitchen is designed to run mostly on your device. We minimize data storage and data sharing.

What We Store Locally

With your consent, we store favorites, sound settings, and app preferences in your browser local storage.

Web Recipe Search

When you search the web, your search query is sent to external recipe providers to return recipe data. We do not maintain our own user account database in this app version.
